About

From industrial precision to digital systems

I spent over seven years working across instrumentation and industrial engineering on large-scale projects — the kind of work where a single overlooked detail can cascade into serious failure. That environment taught me a discipline around QA/QC, systems thinking, and precision that most self-taught web developers never have to develop.

Today, I apply that same discipline to web development, payment system integrations, and AI deployment. Whether it's wiring a Fluent Forms payment flow correctly the first time, or deploying a secure air-gapped AI system, I treat every build the way I was trained to treat industrial systems: test it, verify it, and make sure it doesn't fail when it matters.

I'm currently focused on WordPress development, payment gateway integration, workflow automation (N8N), and practical AI deployment — helping small businesses and institutions get reliable, functional digital infrastructure without unnecessary complexity.
